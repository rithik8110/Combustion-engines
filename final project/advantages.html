What are the advantages of internal combustion engines?

There are many advantages to having and using a internal combustion engines. The most important is high durability and efficiency. High durability is really important when you are going to use an engine in the long term. Another advantage is having high efficiency which means that you won't have to put fuel in that often leading to a less overall cost to keep the car.

There are also different advantages that include:

  1. Comfort - really important in terms of how much you like a certain car or vehicle
  2. This engine can reach high speeds, meaning efficiency of travel is increased
  3. These engines can be used in a variety of industries such as transport of goods and people, hospitality and so much more
  4. Starting the engine is quick and easy to use

There is alot more that I haven't talked about showing why the internal combustion engine is one of the best inventions of its time.
